Privacy Policy — MildlyMindblown Studio

Last updated: 28 June 2026

“MildlyMindblown Studio” (the “App”) is a private, single-operator tool used by its owner to create and publish short-form videos to the owner’s own social media accounts (including TikTok and YouTube). This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Who this App is for

The App is operated by a single individual for their own content channel. It is not a public service, has no end-user sign-up, and does not collect data from viewers or any third parties.

What data the App accesses

  • The operator’s own account access. With the operator’s explicit authorization (via the platform’s standard OAuth login), the App obtains a token that lets it upload videos to the operator’s own TikTok and/or YouTube account.
  • Scope is limited to publishing. The App requests only the permissions needed to publish a video (e.g. video.publish on TikTok, youtube.upload on YouTube) and the operator’s basic account name to display which account is connected.

What the App does NOT do

  • It does not collect, store, sell, or share the personal data of any viewers or other users.
  • It does not read private messages, follower lists, or analytics.
  • It does not use the access for any purpose other than uploading the operator’s own videos at the operator’s request.

How data is stored

Authorization tokens are stored locally on the operator’s own computer (in a private, non-public configuration file) and are used only to communicate directly with the TikTok and YouTube APIs. Tokens are not transmitted to any third-party server.

Data retention & deletion

The operator can revoke the App’s access at any time from their TikTok or Google account settings, or by disconnecting the account within the App, which deletes the stored token.

Third-party services

The App interacts directly with the official TikTok and YouTube (Google) APIs. Use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies and terms.
